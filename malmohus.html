Malmöhus Project

The Malmöhus restoration project focuses on revitalizing one of Sweden's oldest Renaissance castles while integrating contemporary design elements. This conceptual redesign explores how historical architecture can be adapted for modern use while preserving cultural heritage and creating engaging public spaces for visitors of all ages.
