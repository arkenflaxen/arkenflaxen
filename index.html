Sauna Raft

Welcome to the Sauna Raft project, a unique blend of traditional Finnish sauna culture and innovative maritime design. This floating sanctuary combines the therapeutic benefits of a wood-fired sauna with the serene experience of being on water. Built with sustainable materials and thoughtful craftsmanship, the Sauna Raft offers an escape from everyday life, allowing you to reconnect with nature while enjoying the ritual of heat and relaxation. Perfect for all seasons, this project represents my passion for creating functional spaces that enhance well-being and foster memorable experiences.
